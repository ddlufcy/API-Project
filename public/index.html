City Air API

    How To Use

  1. Enter in a city name e.g.(Dehli, St. Louis etc...)
  2. Press Search to see information.
  3. Results and measurements will vary from city to city.
  4. If results are not available an error will show.
  5. Enter a new city to clear results.

What am I seeing?

BC

Black Carbon

 

Black carbon is the sooty black material emitted from sources that burn fossil fuel. It comprises a significant portion of particulate matter or PM, which is an air pollutant.

CO

Carbon Monoxide

 

Carbon monoxide (CO), an odorless, colorless gas, which can cause sudden illness and death, is produced any time a fossil fuel is burned.

NO2

Nitrogen Dioxide

 

Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) is one of a group of highly reactive gases known as oxides of nitrogen or nitrogen oxides (NOx).

O3

Ozone

 

Ozone is a gas composed of three atoms of oxygen.

PM10

Particulate Matter 10 micrometers

 

Inhalable particles, with diameters that are generally 10 micrometers and smaller.

PM2.5

Particulate Matter 2.5 micrometers

 

Fine inhalable particles, with diameters that are generally 2.5 micrometers and smaller.

SO2

Sulfur Dioxide

 

Sulfur dioxide is a gas. It is invisible and has a nasty, sharp smell. It reacts easily with other substances to form harmful compounds:(sulfuric acid, sulfurous acid and sulfate particles.)
